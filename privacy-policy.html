Lawnora is an independent provider-matching platform. This policy does not replace provider privacy policies or service terms.

1. Overview

Privacy Overview

Lawnora helps homeowners submit lawn care request details and compare available local provider options. Lawnora is not a lawn care contractor and does not perform mowing, fertilization, aeration, yard clean-up, sod repair, hedge care, or other lawn care services directly.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected when you visit the website, submit a request form, interact with contact links, or use basic site features.

Participating providers are independent. If a provider contacts you or you choose to work with a provider, that provider may have its own privacy practices, terms, pricing, and service rules.

2. Information collected

Information We May Collect

Lawnora may collect information that you choose to provide through the website, especially when submitting a provider-matching request.

Information you submit

  • Full name.
  • Email address.
  • Phone number.
  • Selected lawn care category.
  • Project details, timing, property notes, access notes, and other message content.
  • Consent confirmation submitted through the request form.

Technical and usage information

  • Browser, device, and general usage data that may be processed by standard website tools.
  • Cookie or localStorage consent preference.
  • Basic form metadata, such as page source and submission timestamp.
Category Example Why it may be used
Contact information Name, email, phone To respond to the request and enable provider follow-up where available.
Request information Service category, lawn condition, timing, message details To understand the request and help match it with relevant provider options.
Preference information Cookie consent choice To remember basic site preferences and avoid repeatedly showing the same notice.
3. Use of information

How Information May Be Used

Lawnora may use submitted information for platform-related purposes connected to your lawn care request.

  • To receive, review, and process your lawn care provider-matching request.
  • To respond to your questions or contact you about your request.
  • To identify which lawn care category may fit the request.
  • To share request details with participating independent providers where available.
  • To improve site functionality, form reliability, and user experience.
  • To maintain records, prevent spam, and protect the platform.
  • To comply with applicable legal, administrative, or security obligations.
4. Sharing

How Information May Be Shared

By submitting a request, you understand that Lawnora may share relevant request information with participating independent providers where available so they may contact you about the service category, availability, pricing, scheduling, and terms.

  • Participating providers may receive contact details and project information.
  • Website service providers may help operate hosting, email delivery, spam protection, or analytics.
  • Information may be shared if required to comply with legal obligations or protect platform rights.

Lawnora does not control independent provider privacy practices. Homeowners should review provider terms, privacy practices, qualifications, licensing, insurance, and service details before hiring any provider.

5. Your choices

Your Choices And Requests

You may choose not to submit a request form. You may also contact Lawnora using the contact details on this website to ask questions about information submitted through the platform.

  • You may request correction of inaccurate contact or request information.
  • You may ask Lawnora to stop contacting you about a request where legally and operationally possible.
  • You may decline non-essential cookie/localStorage preference storage through the cookie notice.
  • You may contact participating providers directly about their own data practices.
6. Security

Security And Limitations

Lawnora uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect submitted information. However, no website, email system, form submission, or online transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

Please avoid submitting sensitive personal information that is not necessary for a lawn care provider-matching request.

7. Retention

Information Retention

Lawnora may retain request information for as long as reasonably necessary to process the request, support platform operations, maintain records, comply with obligations, resolve disputes, and protect the platform.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information, operational needs, legal requirements, and whether a request has already been processed.

8. Children

Children’s Privacy

Lawnora is intended for use by adults seeking lawn care provider-matching information. The website is not intended for children, and Lawnora does not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Updates

Policy Updates

Lawnora may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates may reflect changes in platform operations, legal requirements, website features, or provider-matching practices.
