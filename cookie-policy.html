1. Overview

Cookie Policy Overview

Lawnora is an independent lawn care provider-matching platform. The website may use cookies, localStorage, sessionStorage, and similar browser-based tools to support site functionality, remember preferences, improve form reliability, and manage the cookie notice.

This Cookie Policy should be read together with the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Submitting a request through Lawnora does not book a service with Lawnora. Participating providers are independent and may use their own websites, tools, cookies, or privacy practices if you interact with them separately.

2. What cookies are

What Cookies And Browser Storage Are

Cookies are small files or pieces of information that a website may store through your browser. Similar technologies, such as localStorage and sessionStorage, can also save small preference or functionality data in your browser.

These tools may help a website remember your preferences, keep forms working, prevent repeat notices, or support basic site performance.

  • Cookies may be stored for a session or for a longer period.
  • localStorage may remain until it is cleared by the website or by the user.
  • sessionStorage usually clears when the browser tab or session ends.
  • Browser settings may allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies and storage.

4. Essential storage

Essential Website Storage

Essential storage supports basic website functions. Without it, parts of the website may not work as intended.

  • Remembering whether the cookie notice has been accepted or declined.
  • Supporting mobile menu behavior and basic interaction states.
  • Helping the contact form submit correctly and reduce spam-style submissions.
  • Keeping service request context when a user moves from a service page to the contact form.

Essential storage is used to keep the site functional and does not mean Lawnora performs lawn care work directly or guarantees provider availability.

Lawnora may use third-party resources to load fonts, icons, animation libraries, sliders, or other website functionality. These third parties may process technical information according to their own policies when your browser requests those resources.

7. Manage choices

How To Manage Cookies And Storage

You can manage cookies and browser storage through your browser settings. Depending on the browser, you may be able to block cookies, delete stored data, clear localStorage, restrict third-party cookies, or use private browsing modes.

  • Use the cookie banner to accept or decline the Lawnora site preference.
  • Use the preference controls on this page to update or reset the stored choice.
  • Clear browser cookies and site data to remove stored preferences.
  • Adjust browser privacy settings to limit cookies or third-party storage.
  • Understand that disabling storage may affect some website features.
