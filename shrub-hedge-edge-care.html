Service overview

A Cleaner Way To Discuss Yard Detail Work.

Shrub, hedge, and edge care requests often depend on height, shape, plant condition, access, border style, cleanup expectations, and whether the homeowner wants a one-time refresh or recurring detail care.

Lawnora helps homeowners organize those details before comparing provider options. Participating providers may then explain what shaping, trimming, edging, cleanup, scheduling, pricing, and limitations they can support.

Shape details

Describe whether shrubs or hedges need light trimming, shaping, height reduction, or cleanup.

Edges & borders

Explain lawn edges, beds, walkways, driveway borders, fence lines, and detail zones.

Lawnora does not perform trimming, shaping, pruning, edging, or cleanup directly. Final scope, plant care approach, debris handling, and provider terms should be confirmed directly.

Comparison process

What To Clarify Before Continuing.

Detail work can vary by provider and property layout. Confirm exact trimming, edging, cleanup, and scheduling details before agreeing to any service.

Confirm what plants are included

Ask whether the provider is discussing shrubs, hedges, small bushes, ornamental plants, borders, or only selected areas.

Discuss shape and height expectations

Explain whether you want light trimming, a sharper shape, height reduction, overgrowth cleanup, or a softer natural look.

Review edging and cleanup

Confirm whether lawn edging, bed border cleanup, clipping cleanup, bagging, hauling, or curbside placement is included.

Review provider terms

Confirm final pricing, scheduling, cleanup scope, plant-care limitations, recurring options, cancellation rules, and provider-specific terms.
